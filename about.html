My Story

Growing up, I always knew I wanted to have a career that will allow me to do the things I love: express my creativity and see the world. As a college student, I explored different routes at finding what was for me. Architecture, interior design, horticulture- they were all very interesting, yet none of them actually felt 'right' for me. Then, one day, I stepped into the world of tech. In just a short amount of time, I found my place to be and now I never want to leave.

User experience and user interface designing is what I find pure joy in doing (outside of watching k-dramas and anime). On my chill days, I love painting and being in nature. Hopefully soon, I would like to travel the world and check off all the fun and reckless things on my bucket list. I'm excited for the many projects and opportunities that I'll take on in the future, as well as the different people I'll meet.
